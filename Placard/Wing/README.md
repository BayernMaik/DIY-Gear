<h2>
<a id="id_description"></a>Placard Molle Wing</a>
<a href="#id_navigation" style="color: #dddddd; text-decoration: none">&#x21B0;</a>
</h2>
<a href="./preview1.jpg"><img src="./preview1.jpg" width="100%" ></a>
<a href="./preview2.jpg"><img src="./preview2.jpg" width="33%"></a>
<a href="./preview3.jpg"><img src="./preview3.jpg" width="33%"></a>
<a href="./preview4.jpg"><img src="./preview4.jpg" width="33%"></a>
<br>
This piece of equipment is a modular Wing to scale up Chestrigs with Placards<br>
It is made of a full male Velcro Patch hosting a Molle Panel and female Velcro Patch on the Frontside.<br>
The Webbin0g Loops on the side may host Buckles or D-Rings to attach or stabilize Chestrig-Webbing<br>
The Molle Wing velcros in between the backside and cover of any Placard<br>
